The desired probabilty is 
\begin{align}
    \pr{X \ge 2 } &= 1 - \pr{X < 2 }
    \\
    &= 1-\pr{X=0}-\pr{X=1}
    \\
    &= 1-\comb{15}{0} p^{0} q^{15} -\comb{15}{1} p^{1} q^{14}
    \\
    &=0.1709   
\end{align}
%
where 
\begin{align}
    p = 0.0.5, q = 1-p = 0.95
\end{align}
%
and $X$ is binomial with parameters $\brak{15, p}$.
