\item Let  $X\in \{ 0,1 \}$ and $Y\in \{ 0,1 \}$ be two independent binary random variables. If $\pr{X=0}$ = p and  $\pr{Y=0}$ = q, then $\pr{X+Y \geqslant 1}$) is equal to 
\begin{enumerate}
\item $pq+(1-p)(1-q)$ 
\item  $pq$    
\item $p(1-q)$ 
\item  $1-pq$  
\end{enumerate}
