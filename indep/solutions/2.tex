The  CDF of the X is 
 \begin{align}
 F_{X}(x)=\pr{ X<x }= \int _{-1}^{x} f_{X}(x) dx\\
 = \int _{-1}^{x} \dfrac{1}{2} dx= \dfrac{1}{2} \left( x-(-1) \right) = \dfrac{1}{2}( x+1) 
 \intertext{so} F_{X}(x)=\begin{cases}
 0   &   x<-1\\
 \dfrac{1}{2} (x+1) &   -1<x<1\\
 1  &   x>1 
 \end{cases}
  \label{indep/2/eq:9}
 \end{align}

\end{align}
